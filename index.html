Our tours

Walking Vilnius tour

Classic tour around Vilnius old town to get to know the capital of Lithuania. The tour takes about 2.5 hours but you will talk about it for ages.

Bus tour to Trakai

If you're in Vilnius day trip to Trakai is also a must. Small town with two castles where one of them is based on island on the lake. Tasty Karaite pastries included. We'll also explain you about Karaites.

Pub Crawl

You've seen everything now and you just want to relax and have Lithuanian beer in fun local pubs? We have tour for that too!
